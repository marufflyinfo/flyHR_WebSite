The FlyHR Privacy Engine

Our "Smart Management" philosophy starts with privacy. FlyHR is designed to facilitate high-performance HR operations while ensuring that individual employee rights are never compromised.

Data Encryption All employee records, payroll identifiers, and attendance logs are encrypted using AES-256 standards both at rest and in transit.

Intelligent Attendance

We use ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION and ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION to enable geofenced check-ins.

Unlike standard trackers, FlyHR creates a virtual "Work Bubble." Location data is only recorded when you cross the boundary of an authorized job site, protecting your privacy during commute or personal time.

Secure Media & Biometrics

When you use the CAMERA for biometric attendance or expense scanning:

  • Images are processed locally for verification.
  • Raw biometric data is never sent to our servers; only encrypted hashes are stored.
  • Receipt photos in STORAGE are strictly siloed from your personal gallery.
